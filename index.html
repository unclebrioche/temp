🔥 Harsh Conditions

Strategy: Each condition only needs Rank 1. Run them solo with no other Pact conditions active. Easiest → hardest order below.

✓

✓
Approval Process
2 boon choices instead of 3. Manageable if you stay flexible about your build.
✓
Forced Overtime
Enemies move and attack faster. Easier with ranged weapons or strong Deflect boons.
✓
Underworld Customs
No Centaur Hearts in chambers. Run on a weapon where you can avoid damage — bow or spear work well.

Routine Inspection
Removes a Mirror talent mid-run. Requires Mirror of Night to be fully unlocked first. Do this after completing Dark Reflections.
🪞 Dark Reflections

Strategy: Don't do them all at once. Batch easy ones together, save Stubborn Defiance for last.

✓
Deep Pockets
Starts you with bonus Obols. Zero downside — stack with another from this list.
✓
Privileged Status
+20% damage vs enemies with 2+ curses. Pure upside on multi-curse builds. Combine with Deep Pockets run.
✓
High Confidence
+5% damage per rank while above 80% health. Arthur sword with Spiked Collar keepsake helps stay healthy. Combine with Abyssal Blood run.
✓
Abyssal Blood
Slows/weakens enemies with a Cast lodged in them. Run Arthur sword — strong enough that you won't miss Boiling Blood.
✓
Ruthless Reflex
+50% damage when dashing before getting hit. Gilgamesh fists give 3 base dashes — much easier to trigger consistently.
✓
Stubborn Defiance
⚠ Do this last. Replaces Death Defiance with a single 30% heal on 0 HP. Use Zeus Shield or Achilles Spear with a strong build at low heat.
